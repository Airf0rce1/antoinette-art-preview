Hello, My Darlings

I'm Antoinette Jordaine, a London-based artist working across canvas, murals, and mixed media.

My work is built on bold colour, geometric form, and words that affirm. I believe that art heals, that speaking words into existence really does work, and that a painting on a wall can remind someone that all will be ok in the end.

From large-scale public murals to intimate canvases adorned with gold leaf and Swarovski crystals, every piece carries the same message: you are enough, you are loved, you are more.
