Getting You Found

A simple plan for how the right people find you online, and the few things I will need from you.

First, in plain English

What "SEO" actually means

SEO is just a fancy word for helping the right people find you when they search online.

Someone types your name, or "affirmation artist London", or "mural for my cafe", and we want your website to be there to meet them. Here is how we make that happen, and it is more straightforward than it sounds.

The goal

What we want you found for

Your name

Antoinette Jordaine, your art, your studio. The easiest win, and it lands quickly once the site is live.

What you make

Affirmation art, murals, wearable art, mixed media, and London. So people who love the style discover you.

Ready to commission

Searches like "mural commission London" or "commission an artist". The people most likely to actually book you.

Already done

What is already built into your site

You do not need to lift a finger for any of this. It is quietly in place, waiting for launch day.

  • Your site introduces you to Google as a London artist, in a language Google understands, so you can appear as a real person and practice, not just a web page.
  • Every page has a clear title and description, the exact words people read in their search results.
  • Share a link anywhere (Instagram, WhatsApp, email) and it shows a proper preview with your art, never a bare link.
  • A map of your whole site so Google can find every page the moment we go live.
  • Fast, mobile-friendly, accessible pages, which Google quietly rewards with better visibility.
Over to you

The bit that needs you

Three things move the needle far more than anything technical. None of them are hard, and I will do the heavy lifting on each.

Your Google listingBiggest win

A free Google Business Profile, so you show up on Google and Maps when someone searches your name or "artist near me". I set it up. You just tell me your studio area, a short line about what you do, and a few favourite photos.

The words people would use

A quick sanity check from you on how people actually describe what you do, so we aim at the right searches rather than guessing.

Links from others

Over time, mentions from galleries, press, exhibition listings, and your Instagram bio, all linking back to your site. These build Google's trust in you. I keep the list. You point me to any you already have.

Honest expectations

  • Your name will start showing up soon after launch.
  • The bigger searches (murals, commissions, London) are a slow build over weeks and months, and they lean heavily on your Google listing and links.
  • This is a garden, not a switch. We plant now, and it grows steadily. No overnight magic, but real and lasting.
