Commissions

Antoinette Jordaine creates site-specific murals, interior installations, and bespoke artworks for homes, businesses, and public spaces, as well as wearable art across clothing, accessories, and homeware. Every commission begins with a conversation, allowing the vision, space, and purpose behind the piece to shape the final outcome.

As a guide, public site-specific murals start from £3,500 + VAT, private and residential murals from £3,000 + VAT, and canvas or paper works from A2 size and above start from £2,200 + VAT. Final pricing is dependent on scale, location, materials, timeframe, and complexity, and is confirmed following an initial discussion to fully understand the scope of the project.

A 50% deposit is required to confirm and commence each commission, with the remaining balance due upon completion. This secures your place in Antoinette Jordaine's schedule and allows work to begin with full commitment from both sides.

Collaborations

Antoinette Jordaine is always open to hearing about brand and collaborative projects that align with her values and creative vision. While her standard pricing reflects the time, scale, and craftsmanship behind each piece, she's happy to discuss bespoke arrangements for the right collaboration.
