How they work together

They are not separate tools. They hand work to each other, so the right specialist touches each task.

Voice Guardian sees it all

Number 3 checks anything written, captions, proposals, website words, before it reaches you or the public. Your voice is always protected.

Tech and content team up

Website & Tech (12) and Social & Content (2) share the SEO work: one handles the technical side, the other the words and ideas.

Opportunities flow in

Research (10) feeds the Opportunities Scout (5), who passes the strongest briefs to Business & Growth (4).

Money supports everything

Money & Admin (8) quietly sits behind every deal, commission and brand partnership.

A commission, start to finish
  1. Opportunity found by the Scout (5)
  2. Does it fit your art? Art Practice (1) checks
  3. You decide whether to go for it
  4. Proposal written (4), voice-checked (3)
  5. Contract and deposit handled (8)
  6. You create, then it is delivered and invoiced (8)
  7. Shared on your socials (2 and 3)
An Instagram post
  1. Idea and plan from Social & Content (2)
  2. Shoot planned by Production (9)
  3. You create the piece or the photo
  4. Voice check on the caption (3)
  5. Posted, and results tracked (11 and 2)
